Pink Section

Black text on a pink background is usually perceived as softer, warmer, and lighter. This kind of combination often feels more friendly, gentle, or decorative, especially if the shade of pink is light and not too bright. It creates a sense of something lively, cozy, or even playful, which makes it a good choice for sections with a light and pleasant mood. However, if the pink is too intense or too bright, the text may become less comfortable to read because the background starts drawing too much attention to itself.

Black Section

White text on a black background looks much more contrasting, strict, and expressive. This kind of combination is often associated with something modern, stylish, bold, or even slightly dramatic. It works well for dark sections, banners, headings, or elements that need to stand out visually. However, if there is too much white text on a black background, especially in a large block of content, the eyes may get tired more quickly because of the strong contrast, so it is not always the most comfortable option for long reading.
